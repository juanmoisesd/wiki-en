# 🤝 Contributing to wiki-en

Thank you for your interest in the **Research Wiki — wiki-en** and the Open Research Collection. We welcome contributions that help improve the quality, accessibility, and discoverability of open research data.

---

## 📋 How You Can Contribute

### 1. Suggesting Improvements
If you find errors in the metadata, glossary, or documentation, please [open an issue](https://github.com/juanmoisesd/wiki-en/issues) describing the problem and your suggested correction.

### 2. Research Collaboration
If you wish to collaborate on forensic neuroeconomics or anti-fraud AI research using these datasets, please contact the author directly via [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/juanmoisesdelaserna/) or [ORCID](https://orcid.org/0000-0002-8401-8018).

### 3. Citing the Work
One of the most valuable contributions you can make is to **cite this repository and its datasets** in your own publications. This helps demonstrate the impact of Open Science. Please refer to the [**Citation Guide**](./CITATION_GUIDE.md) for standard formats.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Guidelines

- **Format**: All documentation should be written in Markdown.
- **Language**: We strive to maintain documentation in both **English and Spanish** to maximize international impact. If you suggest a term for the glossary, please provide definitions in both languages if possible.
- **Metadata**: Do not modify `index.html`, `seo.json`, or `_config.yml` unless you are proposing a fix for a technical error or SEO improvement.

---

## ⚖️ Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a professional and welcoming environment for all contributors. Please maintain a respectful and constructive tone in all communications.
