### 🎓 Recommended Formats

#### APA (7th Edition)
> De la Serna Tuya, J. M. (2025). *Open Research Identity & Data Ecosystem v10.0* [Dataset]. Zenodo. https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19415092

#### BibTeX
```bibtex
@dataset{delaserna2025_identity_v10,
  author       = {De la Serna Tuya, Juan Moisés},
  title        = {Open Research Identity \& Data Ecosystem v10.0},
  year         = {2025},
  publisher    = {Zenodo},
  doi          = {10.5281/zenodo.19415092},
  url          = {https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19415092}
}
```

#### Vancouver
> De la Serna Tuya JM. Open Research Identity & Data Ecosystem v10.0 [Dataset]. Zenodo; 2025. Available from: https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.19415092

### 🔬 Citing Specific Datasets
If you are citing a specific repository discovered via the [Data Catalog](https://juanmoisesd.github.io/wiki-en/catalog.html), please use the repository name and the global DOI for the collection:
